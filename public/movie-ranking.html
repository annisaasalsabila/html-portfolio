Annisa's Top 5 Movies

Here are some of my favorite movies!

1. La La Land

With its glamorous musical score, this movie portrays the realities of romantic relationships as they unfold in a realistically imperfect world.

2. The Ring

Inspired by Japanese mythology, the 2002 movie remains one of the most spine-tingling adaptations of supernatural horror stories.

3. Atonement

Adapted from Ian McEwan's novel set in the ush sprawling greens of England, Atonement is as beautiful as it is heartrending.

4. Shang-Chi and the Legend of Ten Rings

As much as it is an action-packed fantasy piece, this movie is a bittersweet representation of what it means to grow up under generational trauma and the love we learn to take from it all.

5. Train to Busan

Not a minute of dullness from this South Korean ensemble piece which is surprisingly adept at portraying the multiple faceets of humanity we find in every single individual.
